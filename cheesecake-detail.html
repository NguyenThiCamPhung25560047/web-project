Cheesecake Recipe

Creamy and rich cheesecake with a buttery crust. A classic favorite!

Watch the Cheesecake Recipe Video

Ingredients:

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 100g butter
  • 900g cream cheese
  • 250g sour cream
  • 200g sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp flour (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 160¬∞C (320¬∞F).
  2. Crush the digestive biscuits into fine crumbs and mix them with melted butter. Press this mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust.
  3. In a mixing bowl, beat together the cream cheese, sour cream, and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add the vanilla extract and flour (if using).
  5. Pour the cheesecake batter over the crust in the pan. Smooth the top with a spatula.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes until the center is set and slightly jiggles.
  7. Let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door slightly ajar for 1 hour. Then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
